I love the State of Michigan. I love the grit and determination of every Michigander that goes to work everyday to build a better life for themselves and their families. I love the fact that I get to wake up in the greatest state in the union and work with men and women that love our peninsulas as much as I do. I love the large (and growing) pool of talented entrepreneurs who call Michigan home and are working to build new companies across every sector imaginable. And I love that I have the privilege of supporting start-ups across Michigan, each and every year, with angel investments.

Reilly and I started Tuebor.org in 2023 because we wanted to make our commitment to invest in Michigan founders, and Michigan based companies, explicit. We wanted to inspire others who believe in Michigan as much as we do to take action to invest in our present and our future.

Michigan has the talent, the work ethic, and the ambition. To achieve our full potential Reilly and I believe we need as many Michiganders as possible to unabashedly commit to investing annually in their neighbors, and across their communities, at whatever levels work best for them.

If you've got a Michigan story — and a passion to make the same commitment — take the Tuebor Pledge today.
