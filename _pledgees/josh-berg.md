Growing up, like nearly all adventurous teenagers, I imagined that I would go off to college and begin my journey of conquering the world — visiting home only to see old friends and to show everyone how sophisticated and awesome I had become since I left Michigan. Well…life has a way of humbling us all and thankfully, my story did not evolve the way I had envisioned. I ended up going to undergrad at MSU and Law School at Wayne State. I met my wife here, built a career here and we are raising a family all within 20 miles of my childhood home.

I have an incredible life. I am so thankful it worked out this way. I LOVE Michigan. I love the people that live here, the resources we enjoy and the community where my family calls home. We live in a place where people make things. We appreciate the sunshine (when it comes) and we take care of each other. We live in the very best state in the Union. I am honored to take the pledge and support Michigan-based entrepreneurship.
