Michigan is my home and also my identity.

Chris and I started Tuebor in 2023 as a long-term commitment to keep making the decision to invest in our people. For me that means backing at least one Michigan company, every year, for the rest of my life.

My friend Paul Pfanner told me once: "life works with commitment and it doesn't work without it." That is exactly the kind of thing Chris and I wanted to embed within the Tuebor idea.

Tuebor is the commitment, and I hope you find your reason to take it too. I'd love to hear when you do.
