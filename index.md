    <div>
      <h3>What is Tuebor?</h3>
      <p>Tuebor is an honor-pledge commitment to invest in at least one State of Michigan-based business per year.</p>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="qa">
    <div class="idx">Q.02</div>
    <div>
      <h3>What is the goal?</h3>
      <p>To increase the number of Michigan investors backing Michigan businesses.</p>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="qa">
    <div class="idx">Q.03</div>
    <div>
      <h3>Are there fees or costs?</h3>
      <p>No. Tuebor is an honor pledge. Tuebor does not collect fees and does not in any way enter the flow of money when you make an investment. Individuals make their own investments independently; Tuebor does not make investment recommendations and is not an investment advisor. See our <a href="/legal-disclaimer.html">legal disclaimer</a>.</p>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="qa">
    <div class="idx">Q.04</div>
    <div>
      <h3>How much do I need to invest?</h3>
      <p>There is no minimum. You could invest in a groundbreaking new Michigan technology startup each year, or simply buy a single share of stock in a Michigan public company — Ford, Hagerty, or MillerKnoll, to name a few. An investment of $10 is the &lsquo;same&rsquo; as one for $1,000,000. All efforts count toward the goal: to increase the number of Michigan investors backing Michigan businesses.</p>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="qa">
    <div class="idx">Q.05</div>
    <div>
      <h3>How do I take the pledge?</h3>
      <p>Start now. Make an investment in a Michigan business during this calendar year, then <a href="/howto.html">show the world</a> you&rsquo;ve committed to take the Tuebor honor pledge — aka supporting &lsquo;Michigan&rsquo;s next sports team.&rsquo;</p>
    </div>
